Digital Resume

Along with my business cards, and resume, I wanted another element for my own personal brand. After browsing for some inspiration, I came across the idea for an interactive or digital resume. I loved the idea, and decided to apply it to my own brand. The colors, fonts, and overall theme match that of my regular resume, but the moving aspect makes it that much more memorable of a piece. The illustrations were all created in Adobe Illustrator, and the actual animation was created using Adobe After Effects.

AutoSpot App Design

The idea for AutoSpot was inspired by a device called Tile. Tile is a tiny Bluetooth tracker with an easy-to-use app that helps you find your belongings like a phone, keys, or maybe even a pet. AutoSpot closely resembles Tile, but for your vehicle instead. Users could register their car or truck, and in the case of losing your vehicle in a large parking garage, or it being stolen, AutoSpot could help you find your vehicle. Once your vehicle is registered, just select to track your car or truck and it will show you where it is on a map. The illustrations and banners were created in Adobe Illlustrator, while the mockups and icon were created in Adobe Photoshop. To create the wireframes, I used Balsamic Mockups.

Sasha Farms Responsive Design

In Manchester, Michigan there is a sanctuary called Sasha Farms. It is the largest sanctuary in the midwest and currently houses over 200 animals of all shapes and sizes. It became an official non-profit organization in 2001, and provides food, water, veterinary care, and shelter for all of its tenants. Being the animal lover that I am, I love the idea of this organization and was really excited to do this project. Because of what the sanctuary stands for, I kept the theme of this project very fun and light-hearted. All the illustrations were created in Adobe Illustrator, with the final web designs being made in Adobe Photoshop. To create the wireframes, I used Balsamic Mockups.

Hollywood Undead Packaging

For many years, Hollywood Undead has been a favorite band of mine, so when given the chance to create a limited edition package for something of my choice, I knew exactly what I wanted to do. I chose to focus on four main elements for this project; the CDs, the CD sleeves, a booklet, and the box. Because there are six members, I chose to put them on the CDs along with the names of each CD. The sleeves feature the CD name as well as a full tracklist. The booklet would include a small bio of the band as well as lyrics for all the songs. The repeating caution tape theme relates back to the style of their music which is rap rock. The CDs, and sleeves were all created using Adobe Photoshop, while the booklet was created in Adobe InDesign.
