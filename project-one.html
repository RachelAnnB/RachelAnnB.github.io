Text Campaign Builder

Role

UX Design/Strategy

Date

2021-2023

OhioHealth

The Text Campaign Builder is a powerful tool designed specifically for marketers to create and store information for their texting campaigns. The tool offers a user-friendly interface that simplifies the process of creating and managing text campaigns. Marketers can easily create personalized messages, schedule campaign launches, segment their audience, and track campaign progress - all within one centralized platform. By streamlining these tasks, the Text Campaign Builder eliminates the need for separate tools and enables marketers to efficiently plan and execute successful texting campaigns. Through its intuitive design and functionality, it empowers marketers to engage with their audience effectively and monitor the impact of their campaigns, ultimately driving better results and customer engagement.
